Refund Policy

If you have made an error in making your donation or change your mind about contributing to Association for Education and Health in Central America Inc., we will honor your request for a refund made within 15 business days of your donation. To request a refund, contact us through email at Billy.Earles@eduhealthca.org. Refunds are returned using the original method of payment, except cash donations will be returned via check. If you made your donation by credit card, your refund will be credited to that same credit card.
