It’s hard to believe, but the third year of our scholarship program is drawing to a close, and when the 4th year begins in January we will be at full rollout! We made the commitment to Ipala at the start that we would add 5 scholarships each year until we reach 20 and then maintain that number. It's just a few months till that objective is met and we’ll be actively supporting 20 students (and their families too!). Our target has always been to help good students that without some help would be unlikely to find the means to go to college or other post-secondary schools. That's what we’ve done and that is what we will continue to do!

The accumulated names may look like just a list, but….

They’re all real people with real dreams of being teachers, business women, architects, and a host of other occupations. Above we have “our guy in Guate” Vernon Archer meeting with the new scholars for 2023 from left to right Melody, Jorge, Marlon, Leslie and Dulce. They all said,

“Thanks to all of you at AEHCA for helping to make our dreams come true!”
